Research Opportunities


STUDENT RESEARCH
Student research comprises an important element of the UTA experience for undergraduates and graduate students. The Student Research Website is intended to provide information regarding regulatory issues and ethical practices pertaining to research, internal and external opportunities for funding research projects, intellectual property, and campus recognition of student research.

FACULTY RESEARCH
Discovery breathes life into novel ideas. UT Arlington scientists, engineers, and other scholars engage in thoughtful exploration and devise products and processes that foster a healthier, safer, and more productive society. The University continues to recruit world-class researchers and add state-of-the-art equipment, generating unprecedented increases in research capabilities and activity.
